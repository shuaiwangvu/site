Data

I work mostly with linked data and knowledge graphs. I perform analysis, study the features and develop refinement algorithms to improve the quality of data. For my PhD, I have also developed tools for fast manual annotation. Throught supervising students, we have generated and studied A.I. generated art.
In addition, I have worked with proofs (in higher order logic).
