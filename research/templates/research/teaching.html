Teaching and Supervision

Below are some courses where I was a TA:

  • Deep Learning

    - Prof. Jakub Tomczak, Prof. Michael Cochez and Prof. Peter Bloem
    - MSc A.I., VU & UvA, NL.
    This course covers various aspects of Deep Learning including Convolutional Neural Networks, Sequential data, Latent Variable Models, GANs and Reinforcement learning, etc. My duty as an TA was to help students with their group assignments and individual assignments.
    See details of the course on its website.

  • A.I. for Health (bilingual)

    - Dr. Annette ten Teije and Prof. Judith E. Bosmans
    - MSc A.I., Wuhan University of Science and Technology, online
    This VU course was adapted and delivered to the master students in Wuhan as a part of the long-term collaboration with the Wuhan University of Science and Technology. I was the teaching assistant for this special bilingual course delivered at remote.

  • Intelligent Systems

    - Dr. Stefan Schlobach
    - First year BSc Artificial Intelligence, VU, NL
    I was a teaching assistant for the Intelligent Systems course, a course that was newly adapted to first year bachelor students. The course covered logical agents, Bayesian network, Machine Learning, etc. I coached two group of 42 students for their assignments and projects.

  • Knowledge Representation

    - Prof. Frank van Harmelen
    - First year MSc Artificial Intelligence, UvA&VU, NL
    I was a teaching assistant for the Knowledge Representation course. I coached in total 43 students and supervised 70+ projects. I delivered 7 tutorials and organised 8 project meetings. I audited the exam and marked 40+ project reports in 2017. Following the change of course format, I was involved in the marking of exams and project reports in 2018, 2019, and 2020.
    Download the lecture nodes, the mock exam, and the slides here.
