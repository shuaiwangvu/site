A Precious Shadow

> A Short Documentary

My duties: It is my honor to have worked with the CinemAsia team for on the FILMLAB project. I worked as a production assistant under the art director Xiang Yu and was the assistant of Amir Bar for sound. I used A.I. for the generation of images for the mood board (see below).

Summary: A dinner conversation with Chinese immigrants from the 90s who worked and lived in Amsterdam's Red Light District. The touching memories reignite a journey of nostalgia at the dinner table, ending with songs about missing home.

Director: Tenzing Woing
