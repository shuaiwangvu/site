LUST

It was a great honor to have contributed to George's poetry collection. Five of my paintings were selected as Illustration for the book.

“Lust” is intended as a poetry experiment that revolves around the idea of physical and emotional attachment and how this can develop in various ways. At times, emotions can be consumed in seemingly satisfying ways, yet it can derogate towards libidinous paths.
